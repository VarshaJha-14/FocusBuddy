FocusBuddy is an AI-powered behavioral assistant designed to help you take the first step when anxiety feels overwhelming. This is not a clinical diagnostic system or medical counselor. If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a qualified healthcare professional or crisis hotline.

How we protect your data

Your privacy and safety are our top priorities.

  • Local scrubbing — Personal info (emails, phone numbers, etc.) is removed from your text before it ever leaves your browser.
  • Server scrubbing — We run a second pass of removal on our servers just to be safe.
  • No tracking — We use local session storage only. No tracking cookies.
  • Ephemeral data — Your vent sessions are not used to train AI models.
