How it works

From freeze to first step
in four moves

No setup. No calendar sync. No onboarding. Just tell us what's hanging over your head.

1

Vent it out

Type or speak your unfiltered panic. Grammar optional. Structure irrelevant.

"I have this stupid paper due and my room is a mess and I hate this topic and I can't even look at it"
2

AI strips the noise

FocusBuddy filters out emotional filler, identifies the real task, and reads your cognitive state.

Core task identified: "Start history essay." State: overwhelmed. Calibrating step size...
3

One absurdly small step

Not "write the introduction." Just the one physical action that costs almost nothing.

"Open a blank document and type your name at the top. That's it. Nothing else."
4

Still stuck? Go smaller

Hit "Too Hard" and the AI generates something even smaller. No judgment — it's just data.

"Just sit down at your desk. Don't open the laptop yet. Let me know when you're seated."
