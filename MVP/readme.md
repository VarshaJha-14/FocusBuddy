# ⚡ FocusBuddy — MVP Landing Page

> A Dropbox-style MVP validation page for FocusBuddy, an AI co-pilot for task paralysis and executive dysfunction.

**Live site:** [FocusBuddy MVP] (https://varshajha-14.github.io/FocusBuddy/)
**Full PM case study:** [github.com/VarshaJha-14/focusbuddy-pm-case-study](https://github.com/VarshaJha-14/FocusBuddy/tree/main/Documents)  

---

## What this is

This is a pre-launch MVP landing page built to validate demand for FocusBuddy before writing a single line of product code — the same approach Dropbox used in 2007 to generate 75,000 signups with just a video and a waitlist.

**The page validates three hypotheses:**
1. Does the problem (task paralysis) resonate enough for users to sign up?
2. Does the video explanation of the neuroscience drive emotional connection?
3. Does a live AI demo convert visitors into waitlist signups?

---

## What's on the page

- **Live freeze counter** — real-time ticker showing seconds lost to task paralysis globally
- **Waitlist signup** — email capture to measure demand before building
- **Explainer video** — the neuroscience of task initiation and why existing tools fail
- **How it works** — 4-step flow from anxiety dump to first micro-step
- **Competitor comparison** — why Notion, Todoist, and ChatGPT don't solve this
- **Pricing tiers** — Free / Premium / Institutional
- **Live AI demo link** — working FocusBuddy prototype powered by Claude

---

## Why a landing page MVP

Inspired by the Dropbox approach: validate the problem and gauge real demand with zero engineering investment before committing to full product development.

**Success metric:** Waitlist signups as a proxy for activation intent.  
**Target:** 100 signups from organic community seeding (r/ADHD, neurodivergent Discord servers) within 2 weeks of launch.

---

Hosted on GitHub Pages. Video hosted on Loom/YouTube.
